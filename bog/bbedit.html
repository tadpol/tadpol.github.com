BBEdit has got POWER!

Never bothered to see what the stuff in BBEdit could do in terms of HTML. I mean, I just assumed it edited text, and had neat coloring. I wanted some automation to my sites, so I tried using Frontier. Boy Talk about killing a fly with a nuclear missle. Saying that it was overly powerful is an underestimation. I don't need that kind of power. (Besides, I have other grips about Frontier.) So I was digging through BBEdits docs, and I start finding out what the HTML tools in BBEdit can do. Lots of neat stuff in there. This should be fun. :)
