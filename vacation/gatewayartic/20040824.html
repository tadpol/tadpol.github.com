Gateway to the Arctic – Day 11

Day 11 — 2004/08/24

This morning we drove out to the Arctic Ocean. Some people swam in, I chose not to, as the wind chill was at 2°F. (I did put my hand in, for what ever that counts.) I was happy enough to just have my picture taken up there.


This afternoon, we went to the airport, and started the ride home from a wonderful trip.
