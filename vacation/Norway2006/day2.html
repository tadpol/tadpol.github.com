Day 2 — 4 Aug 2006

Flew up to Kirkenes early this morning. Gave us some time to wonder about, so we found a little tour to see an old iron mine and the Russian border.

Doesn’t look like much, but the red, green striped post is in Russia. It seems rather normal, I guess I somehow expected something else. (Too many boogyman stories I think.)
