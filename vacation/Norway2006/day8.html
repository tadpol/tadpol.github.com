Day 8 — 10 Aug 2006

Last day on the ferry. Had to be out of the cabin by 10:00, even though we didn’t to Bergen until 14:30. Really beautiful day though; nearly everyone was on deck.

Erling’s uncle, Bjørn, met us at the dock, and we dropped our stuff off at his home. Then we went back out on the waters on Bjørn’s boat and cruised around the fjord. (I think it was Raunefjord, but I’m just guessing based on google maps.) We were going to try some fishing, but time and waves seemed against us, so we didn’t. (I didn’t bring my camera along, so no pics.) After this we had supper, met the entire family and swapped some travel stories and other trivia. It was a fun evening to a day of wondering around the city.
