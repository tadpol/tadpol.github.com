Day 5 — 19 Oct 2007

Short ride on houseboat this morning to get closer to 50 Mile Creek. Again, paddled upstream as far as we could, before beaching kayaks, and hiking a bit further.


After stopping for lunch, we hiking into narrows, and up a ways until we came across a small waterfall. (The ledge there is about six feet high.) Relaxed there a bit, then all the way back to camp for supper.
