Day 7 — 18 June 2005

Couple of us went over to the Harbor Master's office, and made good use of the showers. (A hot shower after a week of cold rainy days feels wonderful.) Then grabbed a picture of the Captain, his family, and the crew. Then off to the airport to head back home.

This was truly a wonderful trip (in spite of the weathers attempts otherwise.)
