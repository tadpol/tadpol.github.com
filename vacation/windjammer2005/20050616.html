Windjammer – Day 5

Day 5 — 16 June 2005

Cool with patches of fog. The captain and crew waited until after breakfast to raise anchor today, that way every one can help. Grabbed a shot quick before taking my turn. Then up with the sails and off we go.


We reached Stonington that afternoon, and went ashore and walked around for a while. Then once all were back aboard, we pulled up the anchor and sailed off a bit to drop anchor for the night.


Since we had the time we went over to one of the nearby islands to walk around a bit. Some people got talked into using the row boat, and they took off with much laughter.
