AmountMeasureIngredient
1/2 cup water
2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
2 tbsp soybean oil
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp lemon pepper
1/4 tsp sugar
1 tsp lemon juice
 
1 1/2 cucumbers -- sliced thin
2 green onions -- cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1/4 red onion --sliced thin and quartered
3/4 green pepper -- sliced thin and quarterd

Mix first nine ingredients well. Add remaining and toss.
